## Secondary or limited sources

These may provide useful leads but should not carry a decisive claim alone:

- FindEnergy county electricity estimate
- News coverage of resident complaints
- Advocacy-group potential-emissions calculations
- Manufacturer marketing claims without project equipment schedules
- Videos and informal field measurements

## Source-handling rule

For every public statement, ask:

1. Is this source about **this project** or only about data centers generally?
2. Is the number measured, permitted, advertised, modeled, or assumed?
3. What year and operating condition does it describe?
4. Does the source include a limitation that changes the meaning?
5. Can the claim be stated in one sentence without losing that limitation?
